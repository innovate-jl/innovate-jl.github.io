Understanding Instagram's Algorithm

Instagram’s algorithm decides who sees your content, and understanding it is key to thriving. It prioritizes:

  • Engagement: Likes, comments, and shares.
  • Relevance: Content that matches users’ interests.
  • Timeliness: Newer posts over older ones.

How to Win the Game

  • Create Saveable Content: Posts that educate or inspire.
  • Engage Consistently: Reply to comments and DMs.
  • Use Trends Wisely: Leverage trending hashtags and audio.
