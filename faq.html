Unifolllow FAQ

How does Unifolllow work?

Unifolllow works by comparing the list of accounts you follow on Instagram with the list of your current followers. If you've used the service before and return with the downloaded file, Unifolllow will highlight new accounts that are not following you back.

Is my data safe?

Yes! We do not store any of your data. Your follower file is processed locally on your device, ensuring your information stays private and secure. No logins or data are saved.

Do I need to log in to use Unifolllow?

No, Unifolllow does not require you to create an account or log in. We believe in providing a no-hassle experience that keeps your privacy intact.

Can I use Unifolllow on mobile?

Yes, you can use Unifolllow on both desktop and mobile devices! Just make sure to download your Instagram follower data and upload it to the platform.

What file formats are supported?

Unifolllow currently supports Instagram follower data exported in HTML or JSON format. Simply download the follower data from Instagram and upload it here.

How accurate is Unifolllow?

Unifolllow's accuracy depends on the data you provide. The more up-to-date your follower list is, the more accurate the results will be. Make sure to download the latest follower file from Instagram before uploading.

What do I do if something isn't working?

If you're having trouble, check out our contact us directly through the contact form. We're happy to help troubleshoot any issues.
