Two Regular College Students

Hello! Welcome to Unifolllow, created by two USC alumni with a passion for making a positive impact. With our backgrounds, we saw a need for a safer, more transparent way to manage Instagram followers.

We believe that Unifolllow reflects our integrity and commitment. Using third-party apps to sign into your Instagram account violates Instagram's terms and conditions. This can often lead to hacked, banned, or restricted accounts. Our platform helps users safely discover who unfollowed them without risking violations and help them understand the data they can legally download from Instagram.

Unlike list comparison websites, Unifolllow is built specifically for processing Instagram files. Simply upload your files, and we will handle the processing on your browser. There is no need to worry about copying the right information.

As creators, we bring different skills to the table but share the same goal of building an innovative and ethical solution. With our tech expertise and understanding of the digital world, we're committed to creating a safer and more authentic online experience for everyone.
